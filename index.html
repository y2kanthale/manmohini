Manmohini Events

Manmohini Events Company was formed in 2017 to bring the talented and legendary artists and musicians to Melbourne so our lovely audience can enjoy quality programs in the best professional format. At Manmohini events we encourage and engage younger generation into this Indian cultural heritage who can take the legacy forward. We would like to say a BIG THANKS to you lovely audience who have shown tremendous faith in us and made our concerts so successful.
