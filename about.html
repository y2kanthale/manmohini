About Us

The Founder

Mangirish and Mohini are extremely passionate couple about Traditional Indian Performing Arts. Mangirish is a unique combination of great organisational skills and a connoisseur in Music and Arts where Mohini is trained Kathak Dancer and teacher. While establishing classical art form such as Kathak, in its purest format here in Australia, they both experienced the dire need of professionally organised quality events. Foundation of Manmohini Events is a result of consistent endeavours made by this enthusiastic couple over a past decade. They have been dedicated towards creating a greater musical community which appreciates Traditional Indian Art Forms. The couple believes in the strength and Class of Indian Traditional Arts. Their vision is to preserve this heritage and extend the love and passion amongst current and future generations in Australia.

Manmohini Events

Manmohini Events Company was formed in 2017 to bring talented & legendary artists & musicians to Melbourne so our lovely audience can enjoy quality programs in the best professional format. At Manmohini events we encourage and engage younger generation into this Indian cultural heritage who can take the legacy forward. We would like to say a BIG THANKS to you lovely audience who have shown tremendous faith in us and made our concerts so successful.
